Portfolio

Full-Stack Development

At the Department of Neurosciences at the Science University of Malaysia, I was awarded $1,300 from the Rome Foundation for the Gastrointestinal Animated Pictograms (GAP) project. To develop a full-stack web application to survey gastrointestinal patients for the GAP project, I connected a HTML, CSS and Javascript front end to a Node.js server using Express.js, integrating to perform CRUD operations on a MySQL database, and deploying on AWS.

Front-End Web Development

As Technical Team Lead at Engineers Without Borders USA, I raised 40% of annual funds to build solar microgrids serving 30 businesses, 2 schools and 1 medical center in Uganda, by using HTML, CSS and Javascript to create and maintain websites for the Columbia University Chapter of Engineers Without Borders USA (CUEWB).

Computer Vision

As Mission Director of the NASA Micro-gravity Neutral Buoyancy Experiment Design Team (Micro-g NExT) at the Columbia Space Initiative, I was ranked among the top 3 finalist teams at the NASA Johnson Space Center Neutral Buoyancy Laboratory, for developing an autonomous camera prototype using Ultralytics and OpenCV to implement a You Only Look Once (YOLO) object detection algorithm with 78% mean average precision, to detect and track astronauts in Orion capsule emergency water landings for the Artemis program.

Robotics

At the Columbia University Robotics Studio, I increased the walking speed of autonomous quadrupedal robots, by applying a Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) algorithm to Robot Operating System (ROS) simulations in Pybullet.
